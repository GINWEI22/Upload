Chapter 1: Introduction

\n

Project Background

\n

The current travel planning landscape is fragmented, making it challenging for users to curate efficient and enjoyable trips.

\n

Problem Statement

\n

Users often face inefficient multi-platform usage and a lack of immersive guidance in their travel planning.

\n

Project Objectives

\n
    \n
  • Implement AI personalization for tailored recommendations.
    • \n
  • Integrate AR for enhanced user experiences.
    • \n
  • Create a unified platform for all travel-related services.
    • \n
  • Incorporate social features for collaboration among travelers.
    • \n
  • Provide offline access to essential features.
    • \n
\n

Project Scope

\n

This project includes the following features:

\n
    \n
  • AI recommendations
    • \n
  • AR overlays
    • \n
  • Booking integration
    • \n
  • Social sharing
    • \n
\n

Exclusions:

\n
    \n
  • Direct payment processing
    • \n
  • Airline operations management
    • \n
  • Hotel management services
    • \n
\n

Significance and Contributions

\n

This project aligns with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) 8, 9, and 11, providing user benefits, business opportunities, and technological innovation.

\n

Project Timeline and Milestones

\n

The project follows Agile methodology, allowing for flexibility and iterative improvements.
