PROJECT TITLE

AR-Traver: Augmented Reality and AI-Driven Tourism & Travel Planning Platform

ABSTRACT

AR-Traver is a next-generation travel technology platform that integrates AI-powered trip planning with immersive Augmented Reality (AR) guidance. This solution streamlines the traveler's journey—from personalized itinerary creation to interactive, on-site exploration—unifying travel information and enhancing engagement. AR-Traver empowers modern travelers with efficient planning, engaging experiences, and supports sustainable local tourism.

PROBLEM

Current travel planning is fragmented and inefficient, requiring users to navigate multiple platforms for research, bookings, and itinerary management. On-site experiences are often static and lack interactivity, resulting in missed local insights and reduced traveler satisfaction.

SOLUTION

AR-Traver is a unified mobile/web application offering:

  • AI-driven personalized destination, itinerary, and booking recommendations.
  • Seamless integration with booking engines for hotels, flights, and activities.
  • AR overlays for real-time, contextual information on landmarks, events, and local businesses.
  • Social features for sharing, reviewing, and collaborating.
  • Offline access and multilingual support.
  • Event and emergency notifications.

TARGET MARKET

  • Tech-savvy travelers seeking personalized, immersive experiences.
  • Millennials and Gen Z tourists.
  • Solo travelers and small groups.
  • Local tourism businesses and authorities.
  • Global travelers requiring multilingual/offline accessibility.

COMPETITION / CONTRIBUTION

Competing platforms (TripAdvisor, Booking.com, Google Maps) only solve parts of the travel lifecycle. AR-Traver stands out by unifying travel planning, booking, AR-guided exploration, and social engagement into one intelligent ecosystem, fostering sustainable tourism and offering actionable analytics to stakeholders.

MILESTONES

Proposed Development Model / Research Method: Agile Iterative Development with User-Centered Design and Prototyping.
